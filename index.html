About

Tatsam is a hindi literary society which focuces on promoting hindi amongst college students by giving them a chance to develop their talent in various hindi literature domains like shayari,kavita and many more. It is one of the most active soceities in nsut which organises regular debate compeititions,group discussions,poetic gathering etc.
